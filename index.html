Our Services

We provide resources and platforms to learners that will help them achieve their goals and dreams.

TeeCha™ - Official Launch; 1 July 2019

This is an application system that selects the most qualified and convenient tutor available for a particular student depending on their area of need. It provides tool for planning and conducting an effective session to the expert and allows the client to monitor the process. The RABBII® admin is ultimately in charge of the tutor and can change him/her incase need be or as recommended by the client to RABBII®.

LyBu™ - Official Launch; 1 August 2019

This is an application system that gives students access to a vast resource of books and papers uploaded by our partners. The client acquires an online library card at an affordable price which is renewed monthly and they select the package they are interested in and thus gain access to the resources.

TuKonekt™ - Official Launch; 1 September 2019

This is an application system that allows learners to interact with professionals or experts who are online through our platform. Any qualified person can act as an expert and answer the areas of concern that learners all across the country have.

Client FAQs

Tutor FAQs

  1. Who can teach?

    2. Every campus student is allowed to register with Rabbii through the link. Your suitability to a student will be matched with what the student desires to be taught. This include the subjects you want to teach, your mastery of the subject and your ability to teach a student.

    After this are verified then you are placed to teach the student.

  2. Does tutoring interfere with my schedule?

    3. Once a tutor with Rabbii® then attendance of classes will be of high priority. However the time set for classes is agreed between the tutor and the client for the convenience of both of them.

  3. Do I get to choose the subjects I teach?

    4. When you fill in your details as a tutor you will get the chance to specify the subjects you will want to teach.

  4. Can I teach more than one subject?

    5. You can teach more than one subject as long as you have a mastery in the subjects you want to teach. You will specify the subjects you want to teach as you sign up. But to every student you are a placed you are allowed a maximum of two subjects.

  5. How do I plan for classes?

    6. The total number of session you will attend are communicated to you by Rabbii®. Days in which you go for the classes and time is planned at your convenience and the convenience of the student/client.

  6. Where do I get resources to teach i.e. books and past papers?

    7. As we begin you will be required to look for books from where you will prepare for your classes. But as we proceed all this material will be made available to you online.

  7. How long does a session last?

    8. Each session lasts a minimum of 2 and a half hours.

  8. What if I only want to teach a high school or a Primary school student?

    9. Your preference is considered.

  9. Which skills can I teach?

    10. Skills are in several Categories. This include Music, Foreign languages, Professional software, Art, Fashion and Design among others. In music there is instruments, vocals and DJ. Under Instruments there is the Guitar, violin, Keyboard etc. You get to choose the skill to teach from this category. (To view the full category visit our blog)

  10. How much do I get paid?

    11. We believe in the value that you impart in the student. A tutor is paid Ksh. 700 for every lesson taught. The terms of payment are stipulated under ‘terms of service’ a document you agree to before beginning your sessions as a tutor with Rabbii®

  11. When will I get a client?

    12. You are contacted as soon as a student matching your specifications is found.

Client FAQs

  1. What is TeeCha?

    2. It is a service where a student is linked up with a tutor tailored to meet the specific academic needs of the student.

  2. Who will teach?

    3. Only teachers and qualified tutors are allowed to teach your child. All tutors and teachers are vetted by Rabbii® and deemed fit to teach a student. Follow up is also done during the tutoring period to ensure objectives are being met.

  3. Can I trust my tutor:
    1. In terms of conduct

      2. We as a company believe in the values we have and the code of conducts set. Our employees also have to live by the values we hold and abide by the code of conduct set for all of us. Therefore every tutor working in Rabbii® shares the same values and thus can be trusted.

    2. In terms of delivery

      3. Every tutor taken by Rabbii® is the best in their respective fields. Coupling this with systems set in place to ensure that a student constantly improves assures of a positive result from the tutor and in the student.

  4. Where will the lessons be?

    5. Lessons are done where the client specifies them to be, mainly at the client’s premise.

  5. Can a female student have a male tutor or Male student with a female tutor

    6. Only female tutors are allowed to teach female students and male tutors are allowed to teach only male students.

  6. When can I get a tutor?

    7. A tutor is made available to you within a day of the client requesting. Request are made through the following link where your details and those of the student are captured.

  7. What if I’m dissatisfied with my tutor

    8. You can contact Rabbii® at any time and your concerns will be addressed as soon as possible.
